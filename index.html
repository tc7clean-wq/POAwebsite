How Online Notarization Works

Step 1: Book Your Service

Choose your service level (Standard $25, Priority $45, or Emergency $75) and provide your information. Payment is processed securely through our platform.

Step 2: Upload Your Document

Upload your document in PDF, JPG, or PNG format. You can also take a photo with your phone. Make sure all fields are filled out before the notarization.

Step 3: Verify Your Identity

Show your government-issued photo ID to the notary. You'll answer a few security questions to verify your identity (knowledge-based authentication).

Step 4: Connect with Notary

Join a secure video call with your assigned Arizona notary. The session is recorded for legal compliance. The notary will guide you through the signing process.

Step 5: Receive Your Document

After notarization, you'll immediately receive your notarized document as a PDF with the notary's digital seal and certificate. This is legally valid in all 50 states.
