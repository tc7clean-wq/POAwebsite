DIRECTNOTARY SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

DirectNotary LLC operates as a platform connecting customers with independent licensed Arizona notaries for remote online notarization services.

2. DOCUMENT RESPONSIBILITY

You acknowledge that:

  • Your document must be complete with no blank fields
  • DirectNotary does not review document content for legal sufficiency
  • You are responsible for the legal validity of your document
  • We recommend consulting an attorney for legal advice

3. RECORDING CONSENT

You consent to audio-video recording of the entire notarization session. Recordings are retained for 5 years per Arizona Revised Statutes § 41-374.

4. IDENTITY VERIFICATION

You agree to:

  • Provide valid, unexpired government-issued photo identification
  • Complete knowledge-based authentication (5 questions)
  • Confirm you are at least 18 years old

5. FEES & REFUNDS

Service fees are non-refundable after successful notarization. Refunds are available only if technical issues prevent completion or notary is unavailable.

6. INTERSTATE RECOGNITION

Arizona remote online notarizations are legally valid in all 50 states under the Full Faith and Credit Clause of the U.S. Constitution.

7. LIABILITY LIMITATION

Our maximum liability is limited to the service fee paid. We are not responsible for document content or legal sufficiency.
